About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced survival RPG where players battle against waves of terrifying monsters. With its unique blend of action combat and strategic character development, this browser-based game offers an immersive experience for both casual and hardcore gamers.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Collect experience gems to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of enemies. Unlock new characters and discover hidden abilities as you progress!
